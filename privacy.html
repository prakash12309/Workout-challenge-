Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Welcome to WorkoutChallenge (we).This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our website and tools.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require user accounts or passwords. The only data stored is your workout progress, saved locally in your browser (localStorage). This data never leaves your device.

2. Use of Information

Your locally stored workout data is used only to track progress and show stats such as completed days or workouts.

3. Cookies

WorkoutChallenge uses only local browser storage for tracking progress. No cookies are collected for analytics or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site may embed third-party videos (such as YouTube). These third parties may collect data according to their own policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for more information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone. All workout data stays on your device.

6. Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored on our servers, your information remains private to your device. We encourage you to protect your device and browser data.

7. Children’s Privacy

WorkoutChallenge is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
